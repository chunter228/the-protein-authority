Smart Snacking: High‑Protein Snacks That Won't Sabotage Your Diet

Snack time doesn’t have to derail your nutrition goals. The key is choosing snacks that combine protein, fiber and healthy fats to keep you full. Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, hard‑boiled eggs and roasted chickpeas are excellent options that provide around 6‑15 grams of protein per serving.

If you’re on the go, look for bars and shakes with simple ingredients and minimal added sugar. Plant‑based drinks like Koia deliver 18‑20 grams of protein plus fiber, making them a convenient emergency snack.
