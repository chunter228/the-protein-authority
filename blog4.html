Ninja Creami and the Rise of Protein Ice Cream

One of the hottest kitchen gadgets on TikTok is the Ninja Creami, a countertop machine that turns protein shakes into creamy, ice‑cream‑like treats. Influencers pour in ready‑to‑drink shakes, freeze them overnight, then “spin” them into dessert the next day. It’s a tasty way to satisfy a sweet tooth while boosting protein intake.

This DIY protein ice cream craze reflects a larger trend: consumers want indulgent snacks that fit their macro goals.
